Process

In 3 simple steps!

1Setup

Plug in IgnoreAds and
direct it to TV.

2Learn

IgnoreAds system learns
how users watch TV and
when they’d like it to be
muted.

3Enjoy

When IgnoreAds detect
undesirable content on users’
TV (beginning of commercial
block) it automatically
mutes TV.
